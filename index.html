Drugless Driving - Impaired Driving Prevention

Drugless Driving is a universal interface focused on preventing impaired driving by inhibiting the operation of a vehicle while intoxicated.
Drugless Driving conducts a breath test compatible with multiple drug detection devices to measure the level of impairment in an individual,
along with facial recognition to prevent cheating. We have a vision where impaired drivers will never be behind the wheel.
